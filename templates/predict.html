    📌 Key Notes

    • 🟠 Sleep Apnea is determined primarily by breathing interruptions, not just sleep duration.
    • 🔴 Insomnia can occur even if a person spends 7–8 hours in bed but cannot maintain deep sleep.
    • 🟡 Restless Legs Syndrome usually causes difficulty staying asleep due to leg movement urges.
    • 🟣 Circadian Rhythm Disorders are related to sleep timing misalignment with the biological clock.
    • 📊 Health indicators such as heart rate, blood pressure, BMI, and daily activity help distinguish different sleep disorders.
