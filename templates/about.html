Advanced AI-Powered Healthcare Solution

Problem Statement

Sleep disorders affect millions of people worldwide, leading to serious health complications including cardiovascular disease, diabetes, and mental health issues. Traditional diagnosis methods are time-consuming, expensive, and often inaccessible to many. Our AI-powered system provides an accessible, fast, and accurate preliminary screening tool to identify potential sleep disorders based on easily measurable health metrics.

🤖

Machine Learning Model

We utilize a Random Forest Classifier, an ensemble learning method that combines multiple decision trees to make accurate predictions. This algorithm is particularly effective for medical diagnosis due to its ability to handle complex, non-linear relationships between features and its resistance to overfitting.
